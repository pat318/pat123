Green Bay to Miss the Playoffs?

Two years ago the Green Bay Packers held a 4-6 record heading into the backend of the season. The playoffs seemed out of the question at the moment, but for them to get there, they needed a little bravado. Aaron Rodgers showed that wouldn't be a problem when he correctly predicted that Green Bay would run the table, earning a playoff birth and a trip to the NFC Championship.
